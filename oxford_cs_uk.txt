University of Oxford - Computer Science (International Entry)
Hard Gates: A*A*A with the A*s in Mathematics, Further Mathematics, or Computing. 
Entrance Exam: All candidates must sit the Mathematics Admissions Test (MAT) on October 21st.
Interview: Shortlisted candidates will be invited for a technical interview in December.
Logistics: UCAS application must be submitted by October 15th. 
Narrative: Your Personal Statement should focus 80% on your academic interest in the subject and 20% on extracurriculars.
