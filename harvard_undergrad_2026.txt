Harvard College - Office of Admissions and Financial Aid (2026 Cycle)
Overview: Harvard seeks students with exceptional academic ability and strong character. Our campus in Cambridge provides a historic setting for groundbreaking research.
The Hard Gates: For 2026, the average GPA of admitted students is 4.2 (weighted). We require either the SAT or ACT; the 75th percentile SAT is 1580, and the ACT is 36. 
English Proficiency: International students must submit TOEFL (minimum 100), IELTS (7.5+), or Duolingo (125+).
The Narrative: Our supplement includes 5 short-answer questions (150 words each). Key themes: Diversity of perspective, dealing with disagreement, and extracurricular impact. Two letters of recommendation from academic teachers are required.
The Logistics: Early Action deadline is November 1st. Regular Decision deadline is January 1st. The application fee is $85. Final high school transcripts are due by July 1st for enrolled students.
