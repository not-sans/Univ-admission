MIT Admissions - Electrical Engineering & Computer Science (EECS)
The Gates: MIT is famously rigorous. While there is no minimum GPA, most admitted students score in the 99th percentile for Math. 
Requirements: Two letters of recommendation are mandatory: one from a math/science teacher and one from a humanities/language teacher.
Themes: We want to see how you collaborate. Our prompt: 'Describe a time you worked with others to solve a difficult problem.' 
Timeline: Deadline for Early Action is Nov 1. Regular Action is Jan 1. 
Portfolio: Optional research portfolio for students who have conducted independent labs.
