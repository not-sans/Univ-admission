UC Berkeley College of Engineering - Freshman Entry Requirements
About Us: Berkeley Engineering is ranked among the top 3 globally. We are committed to serving society through innovation and leadership.
Eligibility (Hard Gates): Minimum 3.0 GPA required for CA residents, 3.4 for non-residents. Recommended high school curriculum: 4 years of English, 4 years of Math (including Calculus), and 3 years of Science (including Physics). Standardized tests (SAT/ACT) are NOT considered for admission decisions.
Narrative: Applicants must answer 4 out of 8 Personal Insight Questions (350 words max each). We look for leadership, creativity, and the ability to overcome educational barriers. No letters of recommendation are accepted for the initial freshman application.
