Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) - School of Computer Science 2026
The Program: Our CS program is famously rigorous. Students have access to world-class labs for AI and Robotics.
Hard Gates: While we have waived SAT/ACT requirements for 2026, we require a strong foundation in Mathematics. International students must provide a TOEFL iBT score of 102+ (with 25+ in all subscores). 
Prerequisites: Official high school transcripts and a secondary school counselor evaluation are mandatory.
The Narrative: We require the Common App essay plus a CMU-specific writing supplement. We look for "intellectual curiosity." Two teacher recommendations are required.
Logistics: Early Decision 1 deadline is Nov 1. Regular Decision is Jan 1. For Fall 2026 enrollment, application portal opens Sept 1, 2025. Application fee: $75.
