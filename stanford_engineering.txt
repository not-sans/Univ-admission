Stanford School of Engineering - Undergraduate Admissions 2026
Eligibility: We look for a holistic profile, but a competitive GPA (typically 3.9+) is expected. 
Testing: SAT/ACT scores are required for the 2026 cycle. 
Prerequisites: Applicants must have completed Physics and Calculus at the high school level. 
Narrative: Our main prompt asks you to reflect on an idea or experience that makes you genuinely excited about learning. (800 words max).
Logistics: Early Action deadline is November 1st. Regular Decision is January 5th. 
Application Fee: $90. International students must provide TOEFL if English is not their primary language.
